# code of conduct

## expected behaviour

Contributors and users are expected to:

- be respectful and direct;
- keep technical criticism focused on code, documentation, evidence, or behaviour;
- assume good intent while still asking for clear evidence;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.

## unacceptable behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- harassment or intimidation;
- discriminatory comments;
- publishing private information without consent;
- sustained off-topic disruption;
- unsafe robotics advice presented as supported project behaviour.

## enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, close, or remove issues, pull requests, discussions, or comments that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocking from the project spaces.
